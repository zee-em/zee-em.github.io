More about our tech and methods

We're using the PICO-8 fantasy console.Fantasy consoles are platforms for making, playing, and sharing tiny video games, with built-in tools for producing art, sound, and code. PICO-8 uses the aesthetics and constraints of classic 1980s video games, sparking creativity while keeping things simple. Its limitations are actually a launchpad, allowing creators to focus on expressive game mechanics and visual storytelling.
